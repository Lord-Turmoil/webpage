- Description -

Greetings, young adventurer!

You accidentally entered a dungeon in the sky, which is full of dangers. However, with the three weapons you possess, you may defeat the enemies and get out of there.

There are three levels of difficulty you can choose in the options. But be warned, they may outnumber you! :)
